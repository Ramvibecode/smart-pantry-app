# Privacy Policy for SmartPantry

**Effective Date: March 29, 2026**

SmartPantry ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Inventory Data:** Product names, barcodes, and prices you scan or enter.
- **Camera Data:** Images of receipts and barcodes (processed on-device for OCR).
- **Authentication:** If you sign in, we collect your email address via Google/Firebase.

## 2. How We Use Information
- To provide proactive stock alerts while you shop.
- To compare receipt prices with product MRPs.
- To notify you of upcoming product expirations.

## 3. Data Storage
We use **Google Firebase** for secure cloud storage. Most scanning data (OCR/Barcode) is processed directly on your device using **Google ML Kit** to ensure speed and privacy.

## 4. Permissions
- **Camera:** Required to scan barcodes and receipts.
- **Storage:** Required to save receipt images for your personal history.

## 5. Third-Party Services
We may use Google AdMob for displaying non-intrusive advertisements. These services may collect device identifiers to personalize ads.
